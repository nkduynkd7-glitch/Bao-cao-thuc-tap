# Tạo VLAN10 và VLAN20 trên port4 và port5

### Mục tiêu

-   Tạo 2 mạng nội bộ
-   Hai mạng ra Internet
-   Hai PC ping được nhau

### Cấu hình VLAN

#### VLAN10 (port4)

Network → Interfaces → Create New
  - Interface: port4
  - Type: VLAN
  - VLAN ID: 10
  - IP: 172.16.10.1/24

<img width="1919" height="946" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8f7b8f9e-a59d-4b85-90b8-9a06206a8fca" />


#### VLAN20 (port5)
  - Interface: port5
  - Type: VLAN
  - VLAN ID: 20
  - IP: 172.16.20.1/24

<img width="1912" height="946" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/010cc70d-6356-4485-8368-91e71572e50a" />

### Cấu hình IP cho PC

PC1

    IP: 172.16.10.20
    Gateway: 172.16.10.1

PC2

    IP: 172.16.20.20
    Gateway: 172.16.20.1

### Policy cho hai VLAN ra Internet
Vào **Policy & Objects → Firewall Policy → Create New**
  - Name: vlan10_to_Internet
  - Incoming interface: VLAN10 (port4)
  - Outgoing interface: port1
  - Source: all
  - Destination: all
  - Service: ALL
  - Action: ACCEPT
  - NAT: Enable
